The Solution

Trisha.AI is an AI-driven platform that automates and streamlines daily business operations for Orangetheory Fitness studios. It deploys specialized autonomous AI "agents" working together - like having a virtual CMO, CSO, CFO, COO, and HRO in one tool.

These agents offload routine work from the owner's plate, allowing you to focus on changing people's lives through OTF's incredible workout experience.

One Conversation, Infinite Capabilities

Studio owners interact with Trisha.AI through just one conversation window - no more juggling between multiple apps, dashboards, and systems. Behind this simple chat interface, Trisha.AI connects to all your business systems automatically.

Powered by Claude's Advanced LLM Technology

At the heart of Trisha.AI is Claude's large language model (LLM), enabling natural conversation, deep understanding of business questions, and contextual awareness of your studio's data.

The Multi-Agent Coordination Protocol (MCP) routes queries to specialized agents powered by domain-specific knowledge. These agents leverage Claude's intelligence to tackle complex tasks across marketing, sales, finance, operations and HR domains.

This architecture creates a seamless experience where studio owners can ask questions in plain language and get expert responses drawing from multiple domains - just like talking to a team of virtual executives.
